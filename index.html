About

An Integration bee is a spelling bee, but you solve integrals.

After the last Integration Bee planned for March 2020 was cancelled due to COVID-19 Dylan we were very upset to see this amazing tradition end. With it being possible once again we decided to host our first integration bee.

We hope that the weather will accomodate an outdoor bee on September 26th, if not we will be hosting the event in the Math Atrium. Check the location tab on this website for updates.

Rules

The structure of the tournament will either be individual problem solving with a 3 minute time limit, a tournament style head to head of who can solve an integral faster, or some combination of the two. We will adjust our plans and choose our style depending on how many people show up.

The integrals will require knowledge of various trig identities and common derivatives, a cheat sheet of some sort will be provided with most of the hard identities. Hyperbolic functions (sinh, tanh) won't be involved in any integrals since it's not in the AP BC Calculus, or Michigan Calc 2 curriculum.

TLDR: If you needed to learn it for Calc 2 you'll need to know it for this.
